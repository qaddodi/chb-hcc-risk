HBV HCC Risk Calculators

Continuous calculation of the image-calibrated HBsAg-HCC nomogram and the published PAGE-B score using shared patient variables.

Research-use approximation. The coefficients below were reverse-engineered from the rasterized nomogram, not obtained from the original model code. Small differences from the authors’ calculator are expected. The figure labels the last axis “7-year survival probability,” although the value rises with total points and behaves like an HCC probability. Verify this against the full paper or author-provided code before clinical use.

APRI = ((AST ÷ AST ULN) ÷ platelets) × 100

Total points ≈ sex + hypoproteinemia + APRI category + HBsAg category + age points
Age points ≈ 1.631 × age − 28.87

Probability = 1 − exp[−exp(a + b × total points)]

PAGE-B
Score = age points + sex points + platelet points
Age: 16–29 = 0; 30–39 = 2; 40–49 = 4; 50–59 = 6; 60–69 = 8; ≥70 = 10
Sex: female = 0; male = 6
Platelets ×10⁹/L: ≥200 = 0; 100–199 = 6; <100 = 9
Risk groups: ≤9 low; 10–17 intermediate; ≥18 high
