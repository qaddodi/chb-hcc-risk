Important: research-use approximation — review model limitations

The HBsAg-HCC coefficients were reverse-engineered from the published rasterized nomogram, not obtained from the original model code, so small differences from the authors’ calculator are expected. The source figure’s last axis is labeled “7-year survival probability,” although its direction behaves like HCC probability. Verify against the paper or author-provided code before clinical use. These estimates do not replace guideline-based HCC surveillance or clinical judgment.
